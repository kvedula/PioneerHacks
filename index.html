What you will learn

In our Hackathon teams have full freedom to hack whatever they want! If it’s a global issue, we want you to solve it! We want our High Schoolers to have complete freedom to explore their interests.

  • Mobile Apps

What we will do

Throughout the event we’ll hold workshops run by experts in the field, for anyone interested to attend. We will hold workshops in the following fields:

  • Wearable Technology
  • iOS Programming
  • Android Development
  • Web Development
About Hacking Generation Y:

We have a great passion for entrepreneurship, and for showcasing talent among youth. We believe that there are brilliant High Schoolers that are ready to tackle the hardest challenges, and we want to expose their talent in our event.
